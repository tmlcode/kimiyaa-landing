About Kimiyaa

An independent 3D platform, built for studios with industry-standard DCC-trained teams.

Kimiyaa is a small, independent company. We're building a platform that takes the workflow studios are already fluent in and reduces the cost and friction of running it at scale.

What we're building, and why

The 3D pipelines used by mid-size studios — typically 50 to 300 seats, often serving Western clients from outsourcing markets — have a recurring tension. The toolset their artists are trained on is mature and capable, but its commercial structure has shifted toward annual subscriptions whose pricing increasingly outpaces studio billing realities. Open-source alternatives are excellent, but they ask for a different kind of cost: months of artist retraining, scripts rewritten, pipeline re-plumbed.

Kimiyaa is an attempt at a third option. A platform that respects the workflow studios are already fluent in — the hotkeys, the file formats, the script style, the plugin patterns — while running on a foundation that doesn't carry the same commercial overhead. Our priority order is: compatibility for trained artists first, pricing predictability second, on-prem AI options for NDA-bound studios third.

We're not a replacement for the major incumbents in the abstract. We're a specific bet on a specific kind of studio, with a specific set of constraints, doing a specific kind of work. If your studio fits that picture, we want to scope a pilot with you.
